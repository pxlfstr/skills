# Pendulum — Dual Animator (Expedition)

Series: **Expedition** (the owner's strongest-preference series). 10 HP · 32 mm · +12 V 80 mA / −12 V 65 mA. Functions: animation, fader, router, waveform generator.

## What it does
Two things in one, both video-rate:

**Dual LFO / animator** — two low-frequency oscillators with **sine, triangle, square** outputs and waveform-selector switches + speed control. Continuous analog waveforms from ~45 seconds/cycle up to frame rate, so it spans slow drift to fast strobing/animation.

**2-in / 2-out voltage-controlled fader + router** — the two fader channels cross over each other under CV, so it patches flexibly as a signal **router/panner, dual fader, VCA, or inverted VCA**. "You can never have enough crossfaders in a video synth."

The clever bit: each LFO can be **mixed into the fader inputs** via dedicated mix controls — so you can run two video-rate signals through the crossfader with modulation already baked in (a self-contained animated transition/wobble).

## Patch ideas
- Slow sine into a shape's position → gentle floating drift.
- Square LFO into the fader CV → rhythmic hard cuts/strobes between two sources.
- Use purely as a 2-channel VCA/panner when you need routing, ignoring the LFOs.
- Cross-patch the two LFOs (one modulating the other's rate) for less-regular motion.
