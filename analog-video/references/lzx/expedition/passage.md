# Passage (Expedition series)

Source: LZX community Legacy Docs ("All About Passage").

**Function:** Triple-channel **wideband signal mixer and voltage processor.** The signal path mixes any signal type — video, audio, or low-frequency CV. Each of the three channels applies a fixed **bias** and **gain** to its IN signal (bias can be inserted **PRE** or **POST** the gain stage via a switch), then sums that processed signal with the channel's **THRU** input. A clean, general-purpose mix/scale/offset utility — closely related to the Cadet VII Processor's bias/gain/thru topology, scaled to three channels.

**Specifications:** Expedition · 10 HP · depth 32 mm · +12 V 40 mA / −12 V 40 mA.

**Per channel:** IN, THRU, output; fixed bias + gain; PRE/POST bias switch. Pairs well downstream of Topogram (sum its band outputs) and with Mapper/Color Chords for color control.
