# LZX Sandin IP modules (DIY adaptations)

LZX produced small (4 HP) Eurorack adaptations of stages from Dan Sandin's **Image Processor** (the IP), the seminal 1970s patchable analog video instrument. Both are discontinued/DIY-era. (Teletect later open-sourced its own IP-Differentiator clone — see `open-source-repos.md`.)

| Module | HP | Function |
|---|---|---|
| **Sandin IP Differentiator** | 4 | High-pass filtering, **edge extraction and enhancement** — pulls out and sharpens transitions/outlines in an image. The classic IP "differentiator" stage. |
| **Sandin IP Function Generator** | 4 | A **continuous waveshaper** giving inversion and level control over the **lights, mids, and darks** of an image (or any signal) independently — i.e. reshape the transfer curve for solarization, contrast sculpting, tone remapping. |

Why they matter: these are direct lineage from the Sandin IP, so they're the historical roots of LZX's edge-extraction (cf. Contour, Curtain, the Visionary TVMF outline outputs) and waveshaping/solarization (cf. Arch, Stairs) functions.
