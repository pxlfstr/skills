# Cyclops (Expedition series)

Source: LZX community Legacy Docs ("All About Cyclops"). Specs/description from the official thread.

**Function:** Interface module that converts Eurorack modular signals to the **ILDA** specification for laser-show projectors (i.e. a video-synth → laser bridge). Best fed by a system rich in VCOs — especially quadrature (sin/cos) sources — plus multipliers and mixers, to synthesize oscillographic patterns and Lissajous geometries on a laser.

**Specifications:** Expedition · 16 HP · max depth 31 mm · +12 V 60 mA / −12 V 50 mA.

**Notes:** No Gen3 successor as of the thread; for Gen3-era laser work, Chromagnon is the suggested path (per Lars Larsen). Relevant to the skill's liquid-light/laser-show topics as the canonical analog→ILDA interface.
