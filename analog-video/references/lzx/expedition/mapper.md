# Mapper (Expedition series)

Source: LZX community Legacy Docs ("All About Mapper").

**Function:** Complex color utility that translates **Hue, Saturation, Brightness** control voltages into component **YUV** (Luma, R−Y, B−Y) and **RGB** (Red, Green, Blue) colorspaces. Effectively an HSB→YUV/RGB color-control converter — a colorspace bridge (related in spirit to Swatch's color-space role, but driven from HSB control voltages).

**Specifications:** Expedition · 10 HP · depth 45 mm · +12 V 100 mA / −12 V 100 mA.

**Patch ideas (community):** pairs with Color Chords and Passage for color control; feed its Y/U/V into Topogram (Y→source, U/V→upper/lower thresholds) for colorization.
